Lasagna

Description

This wonderfull dish has been taught in my family for generations, I learned it from my mother who learned it from her mother and so on for many generations.

The flavours in this dish are unparralled. This lasagna has just the right amount of spice to tickle your tongue and make you crave for some more.

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Tomato sauce
  • lasagna pasta

Steps

  1. Take a big cauldron and add 6 liters of water in it. Heat it up to boiling and add some salt. Once it is boiling, add the lasagna pasta to the cauldron.
  2. While the pasta are cooking, cut the mushrooms in slices. Cut the garlic and the ognions. Scrape the cheese.
  3. Heat up a pan to medium heat. Add some olive oil and put the garlic and ognions in the pan. Cook for 30 seconds to 1 minute.
