Pizza

Description

My best friend and I created this dish back when we were still at the university and we didn t have much money. Therefore, the ingredients are quite affordable and this without taking away any of the good flavours.

As always, this dish is a little spicy but it also has a little sweet touch. It is perfect to eat with a good beer and friends.

Ingredients

  • Pepperonnis
  • Cheese
  • Peppers
  • Mushrooms

Steps

  1. Start the oven to 450 degrees Farenheit.
  2. Scrape the cheese, cut the peppers, cut the mushrooms and cut to tomatoes.
  3. Place the pizza dough on the cooking plate.
  4. Put a layer of pizza sauce on the pizza dough.
  5. Add the other ingredients on top of the pizza and keep the cheese for last.
